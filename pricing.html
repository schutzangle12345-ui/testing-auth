Got Questions? We have answers 🙋‍♀️

Is it really free for 10 questions?

Yes! We want you to see how much time you save. No credit card required, just 10 questions to test the AI drafting and library features.

What counts as a "Question"?

Each unique field in a spreadsheet or portal that requires a custom security response. Repeated common questions (like "Do you have MFA?") are only counted once per questionnaire.

How does the AI stay accurate?

Unlike general AI, testing-auth uses your vetted evidence library (policies, pen tests, audit reports) as the "source of truth." It never hallucinates answers.

Can we add more users later?

Absolutely! The Pro plan is priced per user, so you can start with your GRC lead and add SMEs as you build out your workflow.
